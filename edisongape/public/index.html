What is G.A.P.E all about?

G.A.P.E is all about funding education for those who cannot afford it, especially those in underpriveleged countries. As a rising organization, we work collectively as a group to help students conquer blockades against education in developing nations.

THE THREE PILLARS OF OUR ORGANIZATION

How did we come up with the formation of this organization"?

Due to the compromised state of individuals living in underpriveleged countries, we decided to establish an organization with the main purpose of ensuring marginalized citizens across different communities would have access to proper education, resources, and representation. In the process, we would give people volunteer opportunities by spreading awareness for primary education and allowing people to develop vital leadership skills. In addition, we want to give members of this club the ability to lead with purpose and learn about challenges kids in third world countries encounter. Through this club, we encourage people to help and promote this cause.
